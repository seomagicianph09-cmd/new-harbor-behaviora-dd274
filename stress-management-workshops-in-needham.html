Frequently Asked Questions About Stress Management Workshops

We offer discounted rates for Needham-based non-profits, schools, and small businesses, as well as a 10% discount for Needham residents. Please contact us for a custom quote if you're booking for a group or organization.
How long does a typical stress management workshop last, and can you accommodate evening or weekend sessions to fit busy schedules?
Most of our workshops run for 90 minutes to 2 hours, though we also offer half-day and full-day options for workplaces or special events. We understand that schedules can be tight, so we regularly host evening and Saturday sessions at our Needham location, and can also arrange on-site workshops at your business or organization.
Are your facilitators licensed or certified to lead stress management workshops?
Yes, all of our workshop facilitators are licensed mental health professionals in Massachusetts, such as Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSWs) or Licensed Mental Health Counselors (LMHCs). They also have specialized training in stress management techniques, mindfulness, and group facilitation.
What is the process for signing up for a workshop, and do you offer individual consultations before group sessions?
You can register for an upcoming workshop on our website or by calling our Needham office directly. If you're interested in a private group session for your workplace or organization, we'll set up a brief consultation call to discuss your group's needs and customize the content. We also offer optional 20-minute individual consultations for participants who want to discuss personal stress concerns before the group workshop.
Do you guarantee results from your stress management workshops, and how do you measure effectiveness?
While individual outcomes vary, we use evidence-based techniques and collect anonymous pre- and post-workshop feedback to measure improvements in stress awareness and coping skills. We guarantee a professional, supportive experience and provide follow-up resources.
