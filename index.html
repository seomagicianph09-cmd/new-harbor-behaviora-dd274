Frequently Asked Questions About Individual Therapy

What are the rates for individual therapy sessions at New Harbor Behavioral Healthcare in Norwood, MA?
Our standard rate for a 50-minute individual therapy session is $150. We accept several major insurance plans and can help verify your coverage before your first appointment. For clients without insurance, we offer a sliding scale based on income. Please call our Norwood office for a personalized estimate and help navigating payment options.
How soon can I schedule my first therapy session, and what does the intake process look like?
We typically offer initial appointments within 1-2 weeks, depending on your availability and therapist preference. The intake process includes a brief phone consultation to discuss your needs, followed by completing intake forms online or in our Norwood office. Your first session will focus on getting to know you and setting goals for therapy.
Are your therapists licensed to practice in Massachusetts?
Yes, all our therapists are fully licensed in Massachusetts. Our team includes Licensed Independent Clinical Social Workers (LICSWs) and Licensed Mental Health Counselors (LMHCs), each with specialized training and experience in individual therapy.
What should I expect during my first few therapy sessions at your Norwood location?
In your initial sessions, your therapist will work with you to understand your concerns, background, and goals. Sessions are typically conversational and paced at your comfort level. We'll develop a treatment plan together, and you can ask questions or provide feedback at any point. All sessions are private and confidential, following state and federal guidelines.
Do you guarantee results from therapy, and what happens if I don't feel progress?
While we can't guarantee specific outcomes, we are committed to providing high-quality, personalized care. Progress in therapy varies by individual, but we regularly review your goals and check in about your experience. If you ever feel stuck or uncomfortable, we encourage you to discuss this with your therapist or request a referral to another clinician within our Norwood practice.
