Frequently Asked Questions About Addiction Counseling

What are the costs of addiction counseling sessions at New Harbor Behavioral Healthcare in Norwood?
At New Harbor Behavioral Healthcare, individual addiction counseling sessions typically range from $120 to $175 per session, depending on the counselor’s credentials and the length of the session. We accept most major insurance plans, including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Tufts, and Harvard Pilgrim, which can significantly reduce out-of-pocket costs. For those without insurance, we offer a sliding scale based on income. We’re happy to verify your benefits and discuss payment options before your first visit.
How soon can I start addiction counseling after reaching out, and what does the intake process involve?
We strive to schedule new clients for an initial assessment within 5 business days of your first contact. The process begins with a confidential phone consultation to understand your needs and determine the best counselor match. The first in-person session is a comprehensive assessment lasting about 60–90 minutes, where we’ll discuss your history, goals, and develop a personalized treatment plan. Ongoing sessions are then scheduled at a frequency that fits your recovery plan—often weekly at first.
Are your addiction counselors licensed and experienced in treating substance use disorders?
Yes, all addiction counselors at New Harbor Behavioral Healthcare are fully licensed in Massachusetts, holding credentials such as LMHC (Licensed Mental Health Counselor), LADC (Licensed Alcohol and Drug Counselor), or LICSW (Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker). Our team has extensive experience treating a wide range of substance use disorders, and many have specialized training in evidence-based therapies like Motivational Interviewing and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy.
What types of addiction counseling services do you offer in Norwood, and are family members involved?
Our Norwood office offers individual counseling, group therapy, and family counseling for substance use and co-occurring mental health issues. We encourage family involvement when appropriate, as addiction often impacts loved ones. With your consent, we can include family members in therapy sessions or provide separate support and education for them. We also coordinate with local recovery resources for additional support.
Do you guarantee recovery or provide refunds if the counseling doesn’t work?
While we are committed to supporting every client’s recovery, addiction treatment outcomes can vary based on many factors. No reputable provider can guarantee recovery, as lasting change depends on each individual’s participation and circumstances. We do not offer refunds for counseling services, but we regularly review and adjust treatment plans to ensure you’re receiving the most effective care possible. Our team is always available to discuss concerns and make referrals if another approach might be more helpful.
